    About Us

    • I offer classes which are focused on building strength & flexibility whilst learning to connect to your breath, however strength & flexibility are not required to begin your practice as I will offer modifications for all individuals needs, fitness levels and any personal injuries.

    • Classes are run out of Balmain Rowing Club (BRC), one of Sydney's best kept secrets found on the foreshores of Balmain with uninterrupted water views, perfect serene location to enjoy your yoga practice.
